About PoultryDetect


PoultryDetect is a smart AI-powered platform for detecting poultry diseases using image classification. It helps farmers and poultry professionals quickly identify health conditions and take preventive actions.

PoultryDetect is an intelligent web-based application designed to help poultry farmers and veterinarians detect common poultry diseases through image classification using deep learning. The platform enables fast and accessible diagnosis by allowing users to upload chicken images and get instant feedback about potential infections.
